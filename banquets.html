Banquets

Hotel Tip Top Plaza considers it an honour and a privilege to host your special occasions. Equipped with banquet facilities that can
accommodate from 25 to 2500 guests, Hotel Tip Top Plaza is rightly called a Marriage Multiplex. You can perform an array of
ceremonies at our banquet venues that include:- Engagement and Ring Ceremony, Sangeet, Mehndi, Cocktail and DJ Party, Dandhya,
Marriage Reception, Godbharai, Naming Ceremony, Simant Poojan and many other functions. Hotel Tip Top Plaza is indeed a One
Stop Shop for all celebrations.

We also provide a host of additional services that include: - wedding card printers, interior decorators, ethnic wear designers,
jewelry, beauty salon and spa, photographer, compere, DJ, music band and even a travel desk to book your honeymoon destination.
We meticulously plan and oversee your marriage ceremonies, leaving you with a treasure chest of memories for years to come. Feel
the warmth of Traditional Indian Hospitality at Hotel Tip Top Plaza.

Our halls are ideal for, Birthday parties, Naming ceremony, Ring Ceremony, Engagement,
Thread ceremony, Family get together, Magic shows, Product launch, Press meet, Product exhibitions etc.
